About Me

This is what I do.

Viral Shah

Web and Mobile App Developer.

Viral Shah was born in Borsad, a town in Gujarat, India. He finished his primary and higher secondary education in Borsad. For higher education, he moved to Vallabh Vidya Nagar. As a boy he always wanted to be an engineer or chartered accountant, so he joined bachelor of computer application at semcom college in 2010. During his bachelor he was interested in Computers, particularly, in web desiging and mobile app desiging. In his third years of bachelor he did several project as a part of academics. He achieved Bachelors of Computer Application in 2013.

Then he worked for local company "GeekyWays Pvt Ltd". Where he learned about more interesting stuff like desiging web application and Testing application(manually). His job as a Junior Software Developer involved maintaining, testing, and documenting existing software products.

In 2016, he left this job for higher studies. In September 2016, he enrolled in Mobile Application Design and Development at Lambton College, Toronto to learn more about Web and Mobile Development. Here, he got chance to work in groups for almost all subjects, and gained leadership experience. He also learned latest technologies such as Windows Communication Foundation (WCF), Android, and latest web development tools and frameworks. During this program he, made ecommerce android based application. Currently, he is in the last semester of the project, and he is working as Web Developer with Upzes and as Android Developer with Mobisix.
